Fields of research interest

  • Weak interactions, with specific emphasis on neutrino physics
  • Beyond the standard model, including left-right symmetric model and grand unification
  • Particle properties in material medium, commonly (and inappropriately) known as finite temperature field theory
  • Particle properties in background magnetic fields
  • Astrophysics and Cosmology related to Particle Physics
  • Linearized theory of gravity
