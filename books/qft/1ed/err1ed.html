Answers to exercises

Most of the problems of the book are of the "Show that..." type, i.e., they contain the answer within the statement of the problem itself. There are a few which are not that type. We have received requests to post the answers to these problems on the net. The answers involve a lot of mathematical symbols which are either awkward or unavailable in HTML. So we present them in two formats: postscript and pdf. Click on the format name you prefer to view the answers.
